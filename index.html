About Us

Headquartered in Santa Monica, CA, Addis Global Express LLC provides dedicated, Multi-faceted transportation services, warehousing, order fulfillment services and more from our Torrance, CA facilities. We’re proud of our reputation for efficient work, integrity, and incredible customer service. We have been moving goods for businesses of all sizes; from the small and mid-sized companies. Our reputation is founded on efficient work, integrity, and incredible customer service.

Our employees who drive our trucks and work at our facilities are the heart and soul of what we do. Without them we wouldn’t be where we are today. Because we hire the best employees and treat them right, our clients fully trust the people who handle and transport their goods.

What We Do

Our Services

Service 01

Dry van truckload

Our truckload shipping services include dedicated, refrigerated, private fleet conversion, long-haul, short-haul and regional, expedited, cross-border, brokerage and power only. We also offer less-than-truckload and flatbed services.

Service 02

Port Drayage Services

Our drayage solutions leverage our warehouse and facilitie close to both ocean and inland ports and ramps. As a result, your freight moves seamlessly from port to stores, or anywhere in between. Our TWIC® certified dray drivers can access all U.S. port and rail ramp terminals.

Service 03

Intermodal transportation

The drayage portion of our intermodal transportation include the movement of the intermodal container by truck from the shipper origin to the rail ramp, and also from the rail ramp to the receiver destination.
